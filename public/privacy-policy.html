Privacy Policy for Kitchen Sync

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Kitchen Sync ("we," "our," or "us") is a family organization app that helps families manage tasks, schedules, and shared photos. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information You Provide

  • Family Member Information: Names, email addresses, and preferences for family members you add to the app
  • Task Data: Tasks, assignments, and completion status
  • Calendar Events: Family events and schedules you choose to sync
  • User Preferences: App settings and customization options

2.2 Information from Connected Services

  • Google Photos: Photos from your Google Photos library (only when you choose to connect)
  • Google Calendar: Calendar events (only when you choose to connect)
  • Weather Data: Local weather information for display purposes

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our family organization services
  • Display family photos in the app (when connected to Google Photos)
  • Sync calendar events and tasks across family members
  • Send notifications about tasks and events (if enabled)
  • Customize the app experience for your family

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely using:

  • Firebase/Firestore: For app data storage with Google's security infrastructure
  • Local Storage: For temporary data and user preferences
  • Encryption: Data is encrypted in transit and at rest

5. Third-Party Services

Kitchen Sync integrates with the following services:

  • Google Photos API: To access and display your photos (with your explicit permission)
  • Google Calendar API: To sync calendar events (with your explicit permission)
  • OpenWeather API: To display weather information

These services have their own privacy policies, and we encourage you to review them.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access, update, or delete your personal information
  • Disconnect third-party services (Google Photos, Google Calendar) at any time
  • Remove family members from the app
  • Opt out of notifications

7. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information only in the following circumstances:

  • With other family members you've added to the app (for shared tasks and events)
  • When required by law or to protect our rights
  • With your explicit consent

8. Children's Privacy

Kitchen Sync is designed for family use. If you add children under 13 to the app, please ensure you have appropriate parental consent and supervision.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
